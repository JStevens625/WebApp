Pandas, Things you may not know about them!

You may think you know a lot about a panda but did you know the actual name for the panda is "Big Panda"?

Did you Know:

  • Pandas are actually Omnivores?
  • Baby pandas are only around 15cm big?
  • Only around 1000 remain in the wild?
  • Cubs will stay with the mother for up to 18 months before going out on their own?
  • Pandas do not hibernate?
